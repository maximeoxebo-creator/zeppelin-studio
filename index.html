Zeppelin ships a full family of frosted-glass sections — translucent, blurred panels layered over your imagery. The panels below are the real effect, rendered live.

Glass — Hero, Cards, Key figures, Testimonial, CTA

A whole family of glass sections

Six ready-made liquid-glass sections, plus an optional glass panel on Scattered images and a glass toggle on the Marquee. Each layers a blurred, translucent panel over a background image or color — no code, all editor settings.

Full control

  • Show panel On or off — text can sit directly on the background.
  • Opacity How much of the image shows through the glass.
  • Shape Rounded or square corners.
  • Background Image or solid color behind the panel.

Legible & accessible

Global panel text color with optional per-element overrides for title, eyebrow, and subtitle. The blur falls back to a near-solid panel when a visitor’s device requests reduced transparency — so text always stays readable.
